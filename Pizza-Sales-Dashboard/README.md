\# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard



\## 📌 Project Overview



The \*\*Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard\*\* is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using \*\*Microsoft Power BI\*\*. This project analyzes pizza sales data to uncover valuable business insights through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking.



The dashboard enables users to monitor overall business performance, identify sales trends, evaluate customer purchasing behavior, and discover the best and worst performing pizzas. Raw sales data was cleaned and transformed using \*\*Power Query\*\*, while DAX measures were used to calculate important business metrics.



This project demonstrates my skills in \*\*data cleaning, data transformation, data modeling, DAX, dashboard design, and business intelligence\*\*, helping transform raw data into meaningful and actionable insights.



\---



\# 🎯 Project Objectives



\- Analyze overall pizza sales performance.

\- Monitor important business KPIs.

\- Identify best and worst selling pizzas.

\- Analyze sales by category and pizza size.

\- Discover customer purchasing trends.

\- Support business decisions using interactive dashboards.
