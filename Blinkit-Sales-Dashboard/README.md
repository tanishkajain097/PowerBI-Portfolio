\# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard



\## 📌 Project Overview



The \*\*Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard\*\* is an interactive Business Intelligence project developed using \*\*Microsoft Power BI\*\* to analyze sales performance, product categories, outlet performance, and customer purchasing trends.



This dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, KPI cards, and dynamic filters. It enables stakeholders to monitor key performance metrics, evaluate outlet efficiency, identify top-performing product categories, and support data-driven decision-making.



\---



\# 🎯 Project Objectives



\- Analyze overall sales performance.

\- Monitor key business KPIs.

\- Compare sales across different product categories.

\- Analyze product sales based on fat content.

\- Evaluate outlet performance by location, size, and establishment year.

\- Build an interactive dashboard for business intelligence and decision-making.



\---



\# 🛠️ Tools \& Technologies



\- Microsoft Power BI

\- Power Query

\- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

\- CSV Dataset

\# 📈 Dashboard Analysis



\### 💰 KPI Dashboard



The dashboard provides a quick overview of business performance using four key indicators:



\- Total Sales

\- Average Sales

\- Number of Items Sold

\- Average Customer Rating



These KPIs allow stakeholders to evaluate overall business performance at a glance.



\---



\### 🥗 Product Analysis



This section analyzes product performance using:



\- Sales by Fat Content

\- Sales by Item Type

\- Fat Content by Outlet



These visualizations help identify customer purchasing preferences and top-performing product categories.



\---



\### 🏬 Outlet Establishment Analysis



This visualization tracks sales based on outlet establishment year, helping understand business growth and long-term outlet performance.



\---



\### 📍 Outlet Performance Analysis



The dashboard compares outlet performance across:



\- Outlet Size

\- Outlet Location

\- Outlet Type



These insights help identify the highest-performing outlets and understand sales distribution across different business locations.



\---



\### 🎛 Interactive Filters



Users can dynamically explore the dashboard using filters for:



\- Outlet Location Type

\- Outlet Size

\- Item Type



These slicers allow flexible and detailed data exploration.



\---



\# 💡 Key Business Insights



\- Tier 3 outlets generated the highest overall sales.

\- Medium-sized outlets contributed significantly to total revenue.

\- Regular and Low Fat products showed different sales patterns.

\- Fruits \& Vegetables and Snack Foods were among the top-performing product categories.

\- Customer ratings remained consistent across different outlet types.

\- Outlet establishment trends provide insights into business expansion over time.
